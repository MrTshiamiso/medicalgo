About MedicalGo

A smarter way to connect people with pharmacy information

MedicalGo is a pharmacy support website prototype created to improve access, convenience, and healthcare information for users in Botswana through a clean and user-friendly digital platform.

What MedicalGo focuses on
  • Helping users find pharmacy branches
  • Showing medicine availability information
  • Reducing unnecessary travel and delays
  • Improving digital healthcare convenience

Who we are

MedicalGo is a healthcare support website prototype developed to help users find pharmacies and explore medicine availability information through an organized online platform.

The website demonstrates how web technology can be used to support users in making better healthcare-related decisions in everyday situations.

The problem we address

Many people spend unnecessary time moving from one pharmacy to another without knowing where the medicine they need is available.

MedicalGo addresses this problem by organizing pharmacy and medicine information in one accessible and easy-to-use platform.

Our mission

The mission of MedicalGo is to improve convenience, reduce delays in accessing medication, and support a more efficient digital healthcare experience.

It aims to make pharmacy information easier to access for a wider range of users.

Target users

MedicalGo is designed for patients, caregivers, parents, students, workers, and families who need quick and reliable access to pharmacy-related information.

The platform is intended to work well across both mobile devices and desktop screens.

Why MedicalGo matters

Access to pharmacy information is an important part of everyday healthcare support. A website such as MedicalGo can help users save time, make better decisions, and improve their overall experience when looking for medicine and pharmacy services.

This prototype shows how web technologies can be used to create a meaningful, responsive, and visually appealing solution that supports both users and the broader healthcare environment.

Key strengths

  • Clean and accessible design
  • Clear pharmacy-first navigation
  • Simple medicine availability browsing
  • Responsive layout for different devices
  • Focused healthcare support concept
